Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), Ludhiana, is an engineering institution offering a

comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various departments, including

Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Computer Science, Architecture, and others. The college is affiliated

with PTU and holds NBA, ISO, and NAAC accreditations, reflecting its commitment to quality

education, research, and consultancy. GNDEC provides a well-rounded campus environment with

facilities such as hostels, a computer center, a library, and sports amenities. A key strength of

GNDEC is its strong alumni network, comprising graduates who have attained prominent positions in

diverse industries and government sectors worldwide, excelling as engineers, directors, scientists,

and civil servants. The college website provides detailed information regarding administration,

academics (including a list of departments, academic calendars, and examination procedures),

facilities, research activities, and a showcase of its distinguished alumni, along with details

about accreditations, approvals (like AICTE), and institutional initiatives such as SWAYAM-NPTEL and

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan.
